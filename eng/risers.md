## Introduction

Deepwater risers are conduits used to transport fluids. Various risers are:
- production
- drilling
- intervention

## Drilling

A drilling rig will have a riser type

A riser stack up will contain:
- diverter
- telescopic jt
- tensioner jt
- termination jt
- pup jts
- bare jts
- buoyant jts
- termination jt
- LRMP
- BOP
- Wellhead
